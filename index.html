Ultimatum Flash — Offline File Transfer

Ultimatum Flash is an offline file transfer app that uses your local Wi-Fi network and QR pairing to send files between devices. Transfers happen locally on your network — no cloud upload required.

How it works

  • Open the app on two devices
  • Tap Send on one device and Receive on the other
  • Scan the QR code to pair
  • Transfer files over local Wi-Fi

Use cases

  • Share large videos and folders without mobile data
  • Transfer files when internet is slow or unavailable
  • Local sharing at home, office, or travel

Privacy approach

Designed for local transfers. Your files are sent directly between devices over the local network.
