Ultimatum Share — Offline File Transfer (Android ↔ PC)

Ultimatum Share is an offline file transfer app that uses your local Wi-Fi network and QR pairing to send files Android ↔ Android and Android ↔ PC. Transfers happen locally on your network — no internet required and no cloud upload.

What you can do

  • Android to Android offline file sharing over local Wi-Fi
  • Phone ↔ PC transfer on the same network (local only)
  • Send large videos, folders, photos, documents, and backups

How it works (Android ↔ Android)

  • Open the app on two Android devices
  • Tap Send on one device and Receive on the other
  • Scan the QR code to pair
  • Transfer files over local Wi-Fi

How it works (Android ↔ PC)

  • Connect phone and PC to the same Wi-Fi network
  • In the app, open PC Connect and tap Start (server runs only when you start it)
  • Pair using the displayed QR / token
  • Transfer files locally (Phone → PC and PC → Phone)

Use cases

  • Transfer files when internet is slow or unavailable
  • Move large files without mobile data
  • Local sharing at home, office, or travel

Privacy approach

Designed for local transfers. Your files are sent directly between devices over the local network.
