Privacy Policy

App: Ultimatum Flash: Offline Share (UltimatumFlashShare)
Effective date: 2026-01-14
Support email: flirtyquiz.contact@gmail.com

Summary: Ultimatum Flash is an offline-first local file transfer app. Files you select are transferred locally (Wi-Fi / LAN / QR pairing) and saved on your device. We do not run our own backend servers for file transfers and we do not upload your files to our servers. The app may use Google services (Firebase and optional AdMob) for stability, diagnostics, and sustainability.

1) Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how Ultimatum Flash (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use the mobile application (“App”).

2) Information we collect

2.1 Data you choose to provide

  • Files you select: The App accesses files only when you explicitly choose them via Android system pickers (SAF / MediaStore). Transfers occur locally. We do not upload your files to our servers.
  • Support messages: If you contact us, you may provide your email address and message content voluntarily.

2.2 Data processed by third-party services (optional)

The App does not operate its own backend servers for file transfers. The App may use Google-provided SDKs such as Firebase and AdMob. These services process data on Google’s infrastructure according to Google’s privacy policies.

  • Diagnostics: crash reports and performance data (device model, OS version, app version) via Firebase Crashlytics / Performance (if enabled).
  • Analytics: basic usage events (e.g., screen views) via Firebase Analytics (if enabled).
  • Advertising: ad impressions and interactions via Google AdMob (if enabled), subject to user consent and regional requirements.

3) Permissions used

The App requests permissions only for features you choose to use. Access is user-initiated and there is no background collection of your media.

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES – used to allow you to select and share images locally via Wi-Fi/QR and to display selected images inside the App.
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO – used to allow you to select and share videos locally via Wi-Fi/QR and to display selected videos inside the App.
  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO – used to allow you to select and share audio files locally via Wi-Fi/QR and to display selected audio inside the App.
  • NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES – used to discover nearby devices on the local network for offline transfers.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION (Android 12L and below only) – used only for LAN discovery compatibility where required by Android OS.

No cloud upload. No selling of data. Files remain local unless you choose to export/share them.

4) How we use information

  • Provide core offline file transfer functionality.
  • Improve stability and performance through diagnostics.
  • Support optional advertising (if enabled) to sustain development.
  • Prevent abuse and ensure app integrity.

5) Data sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Google (Firebase, AdMob) as service providers for diagnostics and advertising (if enabled).
  • Legal authorities if required by applicable law.

6) Your files & local storage

  • Files remain on your device: transfers are local only.
  • Storage location: received files may be saved in app-specific folders unless you export them.
  • Control: you can delete files at any time from your device.

7) Legal bases (EEA/UK)

  • Consent: where required for ads or analytics.
  • Legitimate interests: app diagnostics, security, and fraud prevention.
  • Contract performance: providing the features you request.

8) Children’s privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not include user accounts or social interaction features. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9) Data retention

  • Local files: remain on your device until you delete them.
  • Google services data: retained according to Google’s policies and your configuration.

10) Security

We apply reasonable technical measures to protect information, but no system is 100% secure.
